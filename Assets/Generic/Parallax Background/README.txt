Put "ParallaxCamera" on the camera.
Put "ParallaxBackground" on an empty gameObject with children.
Put "ParallaxLayer" on each child of the empty gameObject

Use "Scale With X" to scale them uniformly.

1 means follow camera
0 means stand still in world


Press the cogwheel(on ParallaxBackground Script) and SetLayers to refresh the layers under the empty gameObject that holds all the layers.
